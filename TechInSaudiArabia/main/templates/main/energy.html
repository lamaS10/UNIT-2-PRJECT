About Aramco

Saudi Aramco stands at the heart of the Kingdom's energy transformation. Beyond oil, the company invests heavily in renewable energy, smart grids, and clean technologies, aiming to lead the world toward a more efficient and sustainable future.

Innovation & Knowledge — Ithra

The King Abdulaziz Center for World Culture (Ithra), an initiative by Aramco, stands as a symbol of innovation and creativity in Saudi Arabia. Ithra drives educational, cultural, and technological initiatives that inspire a sustainable and intelligent energy future.

From promoting science and clean technologies to empowering young innovators, Ithra embodies Saudi Arabia's vision for a knowledge-driven economy under Vision 2030.

Smart Energy Projects

Sakaka Solar Plant

Saudi Arabia's first utility-scale solar energy project, producing clean power for thousands of homes.

Dumat Al Jandal Wind Farm

The Kingdom's first wind power plant — harnessing natural wind to generate clean energy efficiently.

Solar Expansion Project

Driving the Kingdom's clean energy future through advanced solar initiatives like the Sudair Plant and REPDO projects under Vision 2030.
