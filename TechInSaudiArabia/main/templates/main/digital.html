Digital Transformation

Empowering Saudi Arabia's future through innovative digital government services.

Leading Platforms in Saudi Arabia's Digital Evolution

Absher

A unified government platform providing 350+ e-services such as passport renewal, residence permits, and traffic violations — making government services easier than ever.

Tawakkalna

Started as a health-tracking app during COVID-19, Tawakkalna has evolved into a comprehensive national platform offering 200+ digital services, including digital IDs, driving licenses, vaccination records, and secure access to government platforms — enhancing convenience and digital safety.

Nafath

A national unified login system that allows citizens and residents to access multiple government services securely with a single account.

Etimad

Managed by the Ministry of Finance, Etimad is a unified financial platform that digitizes all government transactions — from budget management and tenders to payments and contracts — ensuring transparency, efficiency, and integration across public entities.

The Future of Saudi Digital Transformation

These platforms embody Saudi Arabia's vision for a digitally empowered nation, where technology drives efficiency, transparency, and innovation — building the foundation for a smarter, more connected future under Vision 2030.
