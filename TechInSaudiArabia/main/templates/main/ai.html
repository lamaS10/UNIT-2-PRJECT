SDAIA — Saudi Data & AI Authority

The Saudi Data & AI Authority (SDAIA) drives the Kingdom's transformation toward a smart, data-driven future — unlocking innovation, enabling efficiency, and empowering Vision 2030 through advanced data and AI technologies.

SDAIA oversees three key entities that together enable national progress through data and AI:

  • National Data Management Office (NDMO) — establishes policies and data governance frameworks.
  • National Information Center (NIC) — delivers secure digital infrastructure and services.
  • National Center for Artificial Intelligence (NCAI) — drives innovation, research, and AI excellence.
SDAIA's mission: Empowering Saudi Arabia to lead the world in data and AI innovation — building a smarter, more connected nation.
