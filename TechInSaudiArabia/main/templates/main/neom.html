About NEOM

NEOM is a visionary region in northwest Saudi Arabia, designed to push the boundaries of innovation, technology, and sustainability. It is a living laboratory for human progress — powered entirely by renewable energy, reimagining how communities, cities, and economies operate.

NEOM Projects

The Line

A 170 km linear city without cars or streets — built around nature, offering sustainable living with all essentials within a five-minute walk.

Oxagon

The industrial heart of NEOM — the world's largest floating structure, focused on clean industries, robotics, and innovation.

Trojena

A futuristic mountain destination combining adventure, luxury, and sustainable tourism — host of the 2029 Asian Winter Games.

Sindalah

A luxurious island resort and global hub for yachting, marine life, and world-class hospitality — the first NEOM destination to open.

Vision for the Future

NEOM represents the spirit of Saudi Vision 2030 — innovation, sustainability, and opportunity. It is redefining what's possible and creating a new model for living in harmony with nature and technology.
